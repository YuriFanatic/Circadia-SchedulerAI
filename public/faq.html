Frequently Asked Questions

What is this chatbot?
This is a chatbot powered by Google Gemini, a large language model. It can answer your questions, provide information, and engage in conversation.
How do I use the chatbot?
Simply type your message in the input field and click "Send". The chatbot will respond to your query.
What can I ask the chatbot?
You can ask the chatbot a wide range of questions, from general knowledge to specific topics. You can also try having a conversation with it.
Is the chatbot always right?
While the chatbot is trained on a massive dataset, it may not always provide accurate or complete information. Always use your own judgment and verify information from reliable sources.
