Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Circadia SchedulerAI?
    Circadia SchedulerAI is a modern health scheduler and AI assistant that helps you manage appointments, answer health-related questions, and support your well-being.
  • How do I use the chatbot?
    Type your message or appointment request in the chat input and click "Send". Circadia SchedulerAI will respond and assist you.
  • What can I ask Circadia SchedulerAI?
    You can ask about scheduling, health and wellness topics, definitions, explanations, summaries, and more.
  • Is Circadia SchedulerAI always accurate?
    While Circadia SchedulerAI uses advanced AI, it may not always provide complete or fully accurate information. Always consult a healthcare professional for medical advice.
  • Is my data private?
    Your privacy is important. Circadia SchedulerAI does not share your personal information and follows best practices for data security.
